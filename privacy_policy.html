1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our WhatsApp Contact Checker app ("the App") works and handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. App Permissions

Accessibility Service

The App requires Accessibility Service permission for the following purposes:

  • To automatically check if phone numbers are registered on WhatsApp
  • To read screen content only within the WhatsApp application
  • This permission is used solely for checking contact status and does not collect or store any personal data

3. Data Collection and Usage

  • Local Processing: All number checking is performed locally on your device
  • No Data Storage: We do not store, collect, or transmit any personal information or contact details
  • Temporary Access: The app only accesses information needed to verify WhatsApp contact status

4. WhatsApp Integration

The App interfaces with WhatsApp solely to check if numbers are registered. We are not affiliated with WhatsApp and do not access or store any WhatsApp messages, media, or user data. The app complies with WhatsApp's terms of service regarding automated access.

5. Security

  • No External Communication: The app does not send data to any external servers
  • Local Operation: All operations are performed locally on your device
  • Limited Scope: Accessibility access is limited to specific WhatsApp screens only

6. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Significant changes will be notified through app updates. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
