1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the WhatsApp Checker app (the "App") accesses and uses information to perform its functionality. Protecting your privacy is our priority, and we ensure transparency about the limited data interactions handled by the App.

2. Information Collection and Use

  • No Personal Information Collected: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, such as names, contact details, or usage data, outside your device.
  • Local Processing: All data processing occurs locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers or third parties.

3. Use of AccessibilityService API

The App utilizes the AccessibilityService API to interact with the user interface of WhatsApp in the following ways:

  • Read on-screen content to determine if a phone number is registered on WhatsApp.
  • Interact with specific UI elements (e.g., input fields and result messages).

Data Use: The data accessed through the AccessibilityService API is strictly used to enable the app's core functionality of checking WhatsApp registrations. All data interactions occur on your device.

Data Sharing: The App does not share, transmit, or store any data accessed through the AccessibilityService API. It operates entirely offline and processes data locally.
